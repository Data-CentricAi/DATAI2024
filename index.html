The 1st International Workshop on Data-driven AI (DATAI)

Data-driven AI AI Workshop

DATAI 2024

Abstract:

The 1st International Workshop on Data-Centric AI (DATAI) focuses on the symbiotic relationship between AI technology and high-quality data, exploring the latest research breakthroughs and innovative methodologies at the forefront of data-centric AI. The workshop aims to engage researchers in AI and data quality management, fostering discussions on optimal data construction techniques that propel AI technologies forward.

Important Dates:

  • Paper Submission Due: April 1, 2024
  • Notification of Acceptance: April 15, 2024
  • Camera-Ready Papers Due: April 27, 2024
  • Workshop Date: TBA

Topics of Interest:

  • Data discovery for ML
  • Data cleaning & integration for ML
  • Labeling quality and ML performance
  • Data-efficient solutions for ML training
  • LLM-based data cleaning & integration
